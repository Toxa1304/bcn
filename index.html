About us

Relevance

In the 21st century deciding on a career path from the myriad of opportunities and dedicating oneself to grueling years of study has become ever more critical in the lives of young people. Based on our observations, people keep on choosing fields foreign to them based on the pressure coming from family friends or perhaps society or culture as a whole. Our platform proposes an entirely new take on deciding one's career path. Our team understands the process of making better career decisions those decisions based on a carefully planned course of action along with the involvement of human factors, and our platform does both with the intended result would be described as pupils that won't deviate from the intended field.


Description of the platform

Bauska Career Navigator is a matching platform, the aim of which is to assist the pupils of Bauska with a successful career choice. We present pupils with information on Higher education Institutions accredited in Latvia and make it easy to compare to other institutions for a wide range of indicators verified by the educational organizations themselves. Our accessible platform will help students to decide on better educational and professional choices.


Four dimensions of choice

We measure the choice of a career in four different ways

Measure your interest in different fields

Measurement of interest and affinity towards the field.

Match credentials with opportunities

Where does the pupil live and intends on living, comparing educational institutions and their programs.

Financial information

The contents of the programs along with the tuition fees, scholarships and loans opportunities.

Consolation with a professional

Consult with Bauska's career counsellor to reinforce the decision the pupil is set out to make.
