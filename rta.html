Type of institution: State University

Tuition: from 2900 EUR to 3200 EUR

Main areas: Mechatronics, E-commerce, Programming, Interior design, financial management, Philology.

About university

The Rezekne Academy of Technology was created on the basis of the University of Latvia and the Riga Technical University.

In 1992, the idea was to create an independent university. In the same year, on August 28, Congress adopted a resolution calling for priority areas, and all public order was raised in the management of the education system.

In this regard, in 1993, the Republic of Armenia emerged as an independent educational institution. Since 1999, the RA has international accreditation and has become an important cultural, educational center of science.

In 1995, RHEI was integrated into the Rezekne State Pedagogical Institute. Over time, it expanded to four faculties: humanities, law, economics, technology and training. From 1993 - 2015 years, the name of the university was Rezekne High School. Due to the improvement in the quality of education and the expansion of the profile, the Rezekne Higher School was named Rezekne Academy of Technology.

Learning Benefits

The faculty was founded in 1993. It is aimed at training specialists in the field of science, economics, public administration, banking and financial institutions, ensuring high quality professionals in the labor market of Latvia and the EU.

Faculty of Engineering

The faculty was founded in 1993. Initially, the directions of the faculty were: programming, construction, and automotive engineering and food industry specialists. In 1996, another area was opened - the science of ecology.

Faculty of Humanities and Law

The Faculty of Natural Sciences is one of the oldest areas of the Rezekne Academy of Technology. Under the name "Faculty of Humanities," it was created in the early 1991 years.

Faculty of Education and Design

The Faculty of Education and Pedagogy of the Rezekne Academy of Technology was founded in 1995.
