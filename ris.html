HIGH SCHOOL OF BUSINESS, ARTS, AND TECHNOLOGY (RISEBA)

Type of institution: Private University

Location: Riga, Latvia

Language of instruction: English, Russian

Tuition: from 900 EUR to 6000 EUR

Form of study: Full-time, Distance

Main areas: Architecture, Audiovisual Media Arts, Business Psychology, European Business, Entrepreneurship Management.

About university

RISEBA is a higher school, founded in 1992, which purposefully goes towards achieving its goals. It’s located in Riga and its branch is in Daugavpils This school accredited by the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Latvia and has received the International Quality Accreditation from the Central and Eastern European Management Development Association (CEEMAN).

It offers a high standard of education: 1st level higher education, bachelors, masters, and doctoral programs in business, communications, audiovisual media art, and architecture. Students have the opportunity to use the proposed international opportunities for RISEBA.

The vision of higher education is to be recognized by the international university of business, arts, and technology. RISEBA is associated with such concepts as brilliant education, internationally recognized diploma, high-quality educational process, outstanding scientific research base, highly qualified personnel. RISEBA graduates are leading experts in all of Europe!

Discounts and budget places

RISEBA offers applicants to master all kinds of training programs from bachelor to doctoral studies. Also, students showing academic success and showing an active student life can qualify for discounts and budget places.

Budget places are available for full-time students in the following undergraduate programs: Architecture, Audiovisual Media Art, Business Psychology, European Business, Advertising, and Public Relations Management, Entrepreneurship Management.

Erasmus

Full-time students who complete at least one course of study can participate in the ERASMUS program. Students have the right to choose one of the partner educational institutions for exchange training. RISEBA has signed agreements with more than 60 organizations throughout Europe. Students can choose and indicate in the application three priority partner organizations.

Business incubator

All students are allowed to open their own business with the support of the Creative Business Incubator. Students need to develop a business plan during the training period, and in the future, they create their enterprise and start the entrepreneurial activity.
